Embark on an enchanting 6-day journey through Tunisia, where every moment unveils a new layer of beauty and wonder. From the historic treasures of Tunis, including the captivating old medina and the world-class Bardo Museum, to the ancient ruins of Carthage, every step resonates with centuries of rich history. Explore the architectural marvels of Kairouan, where the Great Mosque stands as a testament to Islamic artistry, and Tozeur beckons with its tranquil oases and serene landscapes. Delve into the natural wonders of Chebika, Tamerza, and Mides, where mountain oases and cascading waterfalls paint a picture of surreal beauty, culminating in an unforgettable night under the stars at a desert camp in Douz. Discover the unique troglodyte culture in Tamazret and the grandeur of El Jem's Roman coliseum before returning to Tunis for a final evening of relaxation. With its blend of history, culture, and natural splendor, this 6-day tour promises an unforgettable adventure through the heart of Tunisia.
